How It Works

Our process for helping find missing persons
01

Report a Missing Person

Submit a report with details about the missing person, including their last known location and appearance.

02

Spread the Word

We share the missing person’s profile across various platforms and alert our community to be on the lookout.

03

Receive and Act on Tips

We gather tips from the public and work with authorities to follow leads and locate the missing person.
